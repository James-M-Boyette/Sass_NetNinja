Colors

Classes for each of these colors + attributes were created using 1 map, and a few loops. This kind of pattern is clearly a HUGE time-saver: 11 colors * 2 CSS properties ('color' and 'background-color') = 22 individual classes, and 9 gradations * 2 gammet directions (lighter & darker) = 18 additional classes ... we're already at 40 classes and 2,000 lines of css and we haven't even scratched spacing rules, etc.
